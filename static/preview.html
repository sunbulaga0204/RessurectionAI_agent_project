What is intention (niyyat)?

history_edu
"Intention (niyyat) is the arousal of the heart and its turning toward what it sees as an objective that agrees with its pursuit, either in the present or in the future." Iḥyāʾ ʿulūm al-dīn, Vol. 4, p. 312

Know that intention is not merely a statement of the tongue, but a driving force within the heart. It is the spiritual foundation upon which all actions rest. An action without a sincere intention directed toward God is empty, akin to a body without a soul.

Therefore, one must constantly interrogate their heart before, during, and after an action to ensure it has not been corrupted by hidden desires or the seeking of worldly praise.
